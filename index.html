Levi Ackerman

Levi Ackerman often formally referred to as Captain Levi is the squad captain lit. "leader of the soldiers" of the Special Operations Squad within the Survey Corps and is widely known as humanity's strongest soldier.

Hange Zoë

Hange Zoë was the 14th commander of the Survey Corps, named so by Erwin Smith before his death.[6] Hange was formerly a squad leader in charge of the Fourth Squad, having the duties of innovative inventing and conducting research on Titans,

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager (エレン・イェーガー Eren Yēgā?) was a former member of the Survey Corps. He was the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. He lived in Shiganshina District with his parents until the fall of Wall Maria

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa AckermanMikasa was rescued by Eren Yeager and lived with him and his parents, Grisha and Carla, before the fall of Wall Maria. She is the last descendant of the Shogun clan that stayed on Paradis Island

Armin Arlert

Armin Arlert is the 15th and current commander (団長 Danchō?) of the Survey Corps, named so by Hange Zoë before their death.[10] He is also a childhood friend of Eren Yeager and Mikasa Ackerman.
